About

A Timeline of Migrant Med's Humble Beginnings.

  • September 2015

    An Idea Emerges

    We recognized a gap existed between the wages nurses were being paid and the amount nursing agencies contracted hospitals for their services. We believe in eliminating that gap to allow nurses the ability to negotiate directly with hospital managers for better wages for them and their families.

  • March 2016

    A Marketplace is Born

    Migrant Med's concept was entered into a
    Hack-a-thon where the company idea and
    prototype received 2nd placed and the attention of potential investors.

  • April 2016

    Customer Discovery

    Continued customer discovery was conducted over several months with numerous hospitals throughout the US.

  • June 2017

    Phase One: Registration Launch

    Migrant Med launched to gage interest of potiental users. Initially, we are looking to recruit nurses from all specialties that wish to augment their budget with Per Diem on demand assignments. Later we look to expand to other allied health fields.
